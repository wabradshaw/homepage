PRIVACY POLICY

It's important to know how your personal data is being used on the internet. Here at wabradshaw.com, that's pretty simple. It isn't.

This site doesn't store any personal information or cookes at all. Why would it? Even the IP addresses of my users are annonymised in the logs. Sit back, relax, and enjoy browsing one of the few places on the internet that isn't tracking you.

I do maintain the right to change this privacy policy in the future. I can't imagine a situation where I would though. If it ever happens, I'll do so by updating this page. Should such a change involve personally identifiable information or cookies, I'll ask you before storing anything.
